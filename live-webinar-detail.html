e-Invoicing Phase 2 Extension: What Businesses Need to Know

Get the latest updates on Malaysia's e-Invoicing Phase 2 mandate — understand the revised timelines, how to request an extension, and the immediate compliance steps for your business.

Zen Chow
YYC Executive Director & Country Tax Leader

Description

This live webinar provides an in-depth look at the latest developments surrounding Malaysia's e-Invoicing Phase 2 mandate. With LHDN announcing significant changes to implementation timelines, businesses need to understand what these changes mean for their compliance roadmap.

Join our expert as we break down the revised Phase 2 timelines, walk through the official extension request process step-by-step, and outline the practical compliance actions businesses should prioritise immediately. Whether you are just starting your e-Invoicing journey or are mid-implementation, this session will give you the clarity you need.

What You'll Learn

  • Revised Phase 2 mandate timelines and what changed
  • How to request for extension — official process and eligibility criteria
  • Compliance steps to take now to avoid penalties
